Dedicated Open Workspace

Stay connected to the energy around you while having your own dedicated space. Our open workstations are designed for freelancers, consultants, creative professionals — and even growing companies who enjoy the buzz of a shared environment without the chaos.

With a permanent desk that’s always yours, you get the consistency of an office and the vibe of a coworking hub. Surround yourself with motivated people, enjoy café breaks, and plug into a network that inspires new ideas.

  • Ideal for individuals and small teams
  • Fixed desk with storage and power
  • Access to meeting rooms, Member events and Networking sessions
