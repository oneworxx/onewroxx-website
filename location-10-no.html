Oneworx | 10 No. Market

OW-99 at 10 Number is designed as a modern, design-forward workspace for teams and individuals who want a contemporary work environment in a prime location. Situated close to cafés, retail, and key city access points, this location blends work and lifestyle seamlessly.

The space will feature private offices, open work zones, meeting rooms, and community areas — all curated with a clean, modern aesthetic. It’s ideal for creative teams, consultants, startups, and professionals who appreciate well-designed spaces and a vibrant surrounding.

OW-99 is about working smart, staying connected, and enjoying a workspace that feels current, energetic, and thoughtfully planned.
