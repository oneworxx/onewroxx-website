About the space

From focused workdays to meaningful connections and everything in between.

Welcome to Oneworx, where we provide a dynamic and inspiring co-working environment tailored to help you thrive. With 4 branches in Bhopal, including our latest outlet at 10 No. Market, we offer over 300 seating options, conference rooms, and a versatile event space that can host up to 150 people. Whether you need an interview room, a dedicated podcast space, or even a nap room for a quick recharge, we've got it all. Our play zone and cafeteria ensure a comfortable and relaxed atmosphere, while our secure premises—equipped with biometric machines, CCTV cameras, and power backup—promise a hassle-free experience.

Oneworx is perfectly suited for businesses that operate across different time zones, catering to night offices and global teams. Complementary beverages, housekeeping services, and designated parking add to the convenience, ensuring that you focus solely on your work.

Our co-working space fosters collaboration and innovation among professionals in IT, marketing, advertising, design, and more. It's an ideal place to connect with like-minded individuals and grow your network.

We offer a variety of configurations to suit your business needs:

  • • Shared Workspaces
  • • Executive Dedicated Desks
  • • Private Cabins
  • • Team Cabins
  • • Manager & Director Cabins
  • • Flexible Floating Desks
  • • Managed Offices

Customization options are available to ensure that your office space meets your exact requirements. We are a one-stop solution to all your office-related challenges, offering flexibility, community, and the perfect environment to grow your business.

Let’s connect, collaborate, and co-work together at Oneworx!
